. Destinations
Destination: Bali, Indonesia

Overview: Known for its stunning beaches, rice terraces, and vibrant culture.
Best Time to Visit: April to October for dry weather.
Local Cuisine: Nasi Goreng, Babi Guling, and fresh seafood.
Entry Fee: N/A for beaches, but some temples may charge.
Opening Hours: Beaches open 24/7; temples vary.
Destination: Athens, Greece

Overview: The cradle of Western civilization, filled with ancient monuments and artworks.
Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to October.
Local Cuisine: Moussaka, Souvlaki, and Baklava.
Entry Fee: Acropolis €20.
Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM.
Destination: Cairo, Egypt

Overview: Home to the Great Pyramids and the Sphinx, rich in history.
Best Time to Visit: October to April.
Local Cuisine: Koshari, Shawarma, and Falafel.
Entry Fee: Pyramids around 200 EGP.
Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM.
Destination: Sydney, Australia

Overview: A harbor city known for its Opera House and beautiful beaches.
Best Time to Visit: September to November and March to May.
Local Cuisine: Meat pies, fresh seafood, and barbecued lamb.
Entry Fee: Varies by attraction; Opera House tours from AUD 43.
Opening Hours: Varies by location.
Destination: London, England

Overview: A bustling metropolis with a rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks.
Best Time to Visit: March to May for pleasant weather.
Local Cuisine: Fish and chips, Full English breakfast, and afternoon tea.
Entry Fee: Varies; many museums are free.
Opening Hours: Generally 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.
Destination: Dubai, UAE

Overview: A modern city known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and lively nightlife.
Best Time to Visit: November to March.
Local Cuisine: Shawarma, Hummus, and Falafel.
Entry Fee: Burj Khalifa around AED 149.
Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM.
Destination: Kyoto, Japan

Overview: Known for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses.
Best Time to Visit: March to May for cherry blossoms; October for autumn colors.
Local Cuisine: Kaiseki, Yudofu, and Matcha sweets.
Entry Fee: Varies by temple; around ¥500.
Opening Hours: Varies by location.
Destination: New York City, USA

Overview: The city that never sleeps, filled with iconic landmarks and diverse neighborhoods.
Best Time to Visit: Spring (April to June) and Fall (September to November).
Local Cuisine: Bagels, New York-style pizza, and cheesecake.
Entry Fee: Varies; some parks free, museums may charge.
Opening Hours: Varies by attraction.
Destination: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Overview: Famous for its Copacabana beach, Sugarloaf Mountain, and vibrant Carnival festival.
Best Time to Visit: December to March for summer.
Local Cuisine: Feijoada, Pão de Queijo, and Caipirinha.
Entry Fee: Free for beaches; attractions vary.
Opening Hours: Varies by location.
Destination: Vancouver, Canada

Overview: Known for its stunning natural scenery, including mountains and ocean views.
Best Time to Visit: May to September.
Local Cuisine: Salmon, Poutine, and Asian cuisine.
Entry Fee: Free access to parks; attractions vary.
Opening Hours: Varies by attraction.
2. City Tours

City Tour: Rome, Italy

City Tour: Bangkok, Thailand

City Tour: San Francisco, USA

Duration: 3 days

3. Tourist Attractions
Attraction: Machu Picchu, Peru

Description: A 15th-century Inca citadel set high in the Andes Mountains, known for its archaeological significance.
Entry Fee: $50; includes access to the site.
Opening Hours: 6:00 AM - 5:30 PM.
Attraction: Niagara Falls, USA/Canada

Description: Iconic waterfalls located on the border between the U.S. and Canada; famous for their beauty and power.
Entry Fee: Free to view; boat tours available at $25.
Best Time to Visit: Summer for the full experience; winter for ice formations.
Attraction: Petra, Jordan

Description: Ancient city famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system, recognized as one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
Entry Fee: 50 JD; valid for one day.
Opening Hours: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM.
Attraction: Stonehenge, England

Description: Prehistoric monument consisting of a ring of standing stones; a UNESCO World Heritage site.
Entry Fee: £19.50; includes visitor center.
Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM.
Attraction: Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Description: The largest religious monument in the world, originally constructed as a Hindu temple, later transformed into a Buddhist temple.
Entry Fee: $37 for one day.
Opening Hours: 5:00 AM - 6:00 PM.
4. Travel Itineraries
Itinerary: 10-Day European Adventure

Day 1: Fly to London; visit the Tower of London.
Day 2: Explore Buckingham Palace and the British Museum.
Day 3: Train to Paris; visit the Eiffel Tower.
Day 4: Day trip to Versailles.
Day 5: Fly to Rome; explore the Colosseum.
Day 6: Visit Vatican City and St. Peter's Basilica.
Day 7: Train to Florence; see the Duomo.
Day 8: Day trip to Pisa.
Day 9: Fly to Barcelona; visit La Sagrada Familia.
Day 10: Relax at the beach before flying home.
Itinerary: 7-Day Italian Escape

Day 1: Arrive in Venice; gondola ride.
Day 2: Explore St. Mark’s Basilica.
Day 3: Train to Florence; visit the Uffizi Gallery.
Day 4: Day trip to Siena.
Day 5: Train to Rome; visit the Roman Forum.
Day 6: Explore Trastevere and enjoy local cuisine.
Day 7: Fly home.
Itinerary: 5-Day New York City Tour

Day 1: Arrive; visit Times Square.
Day 2: Explore Central Park and museums.
Day 3: Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.
Day 4: Broadway show and shopping in Soho.
Day 5: Brooklyn Bridge and return home.
